Privacy Policy for Olivewood Facilities Booking Application

This application collects limited personal information. Specifically, your name, house unit number, and phone number. These details are required to identify the account under which bookings for the sports facilities are made and to ensure accurate facility scheduling.

Information Collected

We collect your name, house unit number, and phone number when you create your account.

Purpose of Collection

This information is used solely to identify users making bookings, to ensure each user can interact with their own bookings. It is not used for marketing, analytics, or any other purpose.

Data Storage

Your information is stored securely in our backend database. We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.

Data Usage

The data is only used to provide app functionality (booking identification and creation). We do not generate revenue from your information.

User Rights

You may update your information in the settings of the application. If you wish to delete your account, you may contact us to do so.
